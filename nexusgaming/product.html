about this game:

Dota 2, a 2013 MOBA game by Valve, is a sequel to the community-created mod Defense of the Ancients (Dota) for Warcraft III. In Dota 2, two teams of five players defend their bases while controlling unique heroes with distinct abilities. Players gain experience and items to enhance their heroes and aim to destroy the opposing team's Ancient to win.
